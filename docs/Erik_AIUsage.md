## AI-Usage
* FXML indentation
  * With the usage of SceneBuilder the indentations are sometimes all over the place and thereby the fxml is hard to read.
  * The cleanup takes a long time if you do it yourself. LLMs are just faster and the time you get by not having to care about the cleanup can be used for more relevant (coding) stuff

* CSS-Color Schemes
  * While I try my best to find matching color combinations the AI has more information about matching colors.
  * With the help of AI the UI has a good-looking color scheme

* Help with Bugfixes
  * While for some Bugs it's easy to pinpoint why and where they happen this is not the case for all. In those cases where I was not able to pinpoint the reason for a Bug or why the Bug occurred LLMs can sometimes help.
